About Us

Empowering women. Elevating poultry. Serving Mizoram—fresh, safe, and fair.

Chhimphei Women Poultry Producer Company Limited (WPPCL) is the first women poultry producer company of Mizoram, established in 2018. We are committed to transforming the livelihoods of smallholder producers through a pro-poor poultry value chain—helping farmers upgrade practices and thrive in dynamic markets.
